GOAL

The app that I will be implementing is a note taking app that is inclined towards students from either school or univerisities. Some of the features include:

  • Timetable which can be set by the student or administration of the school/university
  • Automatic subject selection which will be based on the timetable
  • Ability to add images to the notes
