GOAL

The app that I will be implementing is a note taking app that is inclined towards students from either schools or univerisities. Some of the features include:

  • Calendar which will have the timetable and can be set by the student or administration of the school/university.
  • Automatic subject selection which will be based on the calendar.
  • Ability to add images and recordings to the notes.
  • Reminder page for adding reminders for tests, quizes, etc, which can be added by the teacher or student.

The app fixes the problems of note taking that students have as there aren't many if not no apps that are specific for students. Notamatic may not have the most sophisticated features, but the simplified UI and necessary features make it appealing to students. Furthermore, it also focuses on teachers and student collaboration as it is crucial for effective learning.
